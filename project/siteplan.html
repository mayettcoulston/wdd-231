Nairobi Eats Site Plan

Local Food & Dining Guide

Site Name

Nairobi Eats

The name “Nairobi Eats” was selected because it clearly represents a food-focused website centered on Nairobi. It is short, memorable, and directly communicates the purpose of the site—discovering food and dining experiences in the city.

Site Purpose

Nairobi Eats is an informational website designed to showcase Nairobi’s diverse food scene. The site provides users with information about popular local cuisines, recommended restaurants, and the cultural background of Kenyan food. It promotes local eateries while offering an engaging and easy-to-use browsing experience.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the best local restaurants serving authentic Kenyan food?
  • What traditional dishes should I try when visiting Nairobi for the first time?

Color Scheme

The following colors are used throughout the site plan and will be used consistently across the website.

Primary Green (#2E5339) Headings, navigation, accents

Secondary Gold (#C17817) Highlights, buttons, borders

Background Beige (#F2E9D0) Page background

Typography

  • Poppins Used for headings (h1, h2, h3)
  • Roboto Used for body text and paragraphs
